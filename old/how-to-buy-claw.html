Step 2: Download a crypto wallet and connect it to BSC

Hardware wallets are great for crypto investors looking to securely store their crypto assets offline while being able to use them online aswell.
In order to use Pancakeswap to buy CLAW, you'll need to use a software wallet that is compatible to the exchange.
Trustwallet and Metamask are good options, but you must enable them to work with the Binance Smart Chain
Trustwallet and Metamask are good options, but you must enable them to work with the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) before using it for these tokens.
In this example we will use Metamask on a PC as crypto wallet to connect to the BSC.

Most cryptocurrency users who interact with decentralized exchanges use Metamask wallet.
as its Google Chrome and FireFox extensions make it easy to use with other programs on your PC.
To do this, you'll need to install the extension and open it. once you've done that, you'll need to open the network drop down in the top right.
This is most likely set to "Main network". In this dropdown menu scroll down and click "costum RFP" and fill in the following information.
1. Network name: Binance Smart Chain
2. New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed1.binance.org:443
3. ChainID: 56
4. Symbol: BNB
5. Block Explorer: https://explorer.binance.org/smart
Once you've made your Metamask compatible with BSC, youre ready to make this purchase on Pancakeswap.

Step 3: Make your purchase!

Suprisingly, buying CLAW on pancakeswap is a much more simple process than setting up your crypto wallet and binance account.

This way, all you need to do is follow this link:
https://exchange.pancakeswap.finance/#/swap?outputCurrency=0x18a323b43a06c1319168f522513f318391f8cac5
And then just connect your wallet to Pancakeswap to buy CLAW!
All you need to do now is put in the amount of BNB you want to use to buy CLAW, up the slippage tolerance to 10-13% and buy!
